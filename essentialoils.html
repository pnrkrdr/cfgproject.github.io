Essential Oils

Rosa Damascena

A sweet, soft-floral scent. Heart-warming & sensual. A very mild & safe oil. Invaluable for sensitive & mature skin care with rejuvenating qualities. Eases depression & nervous tension.

Citrus Limon

Light, refreshing citrus scent. Purifying & refreshing. Excellent detox aid. Toning & stimulating. Anti-cellulite. Good for oily & blemished skin care. Uplifting room freshener.

Mentha Piperita

Fresh grassy-minty scent. Cooling & refreshing. Eases respiratory congestion. Good for colds & chills. Anti-travel sickness. Helps combats jet lag..

Lavandula Officinalis

Familiar, sweet-floral scent. Relaxing, healing & soothing versatile remedy. Invaluable for sensitive & after-sun skin care. Relief for minor burns & insect bites. Eases nervous tension & headaches: one of the best all round anti-stress remedies.
